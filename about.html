ABOUT OUR BUSINESS

It all started back in 2018, when I got my first record and record player for Christmas. Billy Idol’s “Don’t Stop” EP. I was eager to buy and collect more, searching countless hours through bins at second hand stores and flea markets for vintage records by older artists.

Fast-forward to 2022, Little Black Disc has begun its journey. We want to give our customers that same first-record feeling as I felt four years ago.
